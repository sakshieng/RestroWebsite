About Us

1. Search Restaurants
Search by restaurant name, cuisine type, city, or zip code. You can even quickly find "deals near me" in our mobile app. Not only can Restaurant.com help you save on the road, it’s a great dining guide!

2. Choose Your Savings
Once you’ve discovered the perfect restaurant, it’s time to select a certificate. Dining in a group? Get $25 or $50 off your next meal. Grabbing a quick lunch or dinner for two? Why not save $10 or $15 at your favorite spot.

3. Dine & Save
It’s time to dine! Present your certificate to the server on your mobile phone, or bring a printed copy, and voilà – the savings will appear on your final bill.
